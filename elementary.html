Elementary and Kindergarten

Website of Elementary and Kindergarden of city Bohuslavice, Czech Republic.

Project overview

As part of this project, I was tasked with redesigning an outdated website and adding new features to enhance its functionality. To accomplish this, I utilized a variety of tools and techniques, including WordPress, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Using WordPress as the content management system, I implemented the new design, incorporating custom HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to add additional features and functionality. These enhancements included:
• Responsive design for mobile devices
• Improved navigation and site structure
• Integration of third-party plugins to extend site capabilities
• Custom widgets and shortcodes to improve user experience
• Enhanced security measures to protect against online threats

Throughout the project, I collaborated closely with the client to ensure that the site met their specific needs and goals. As a result of my efforts, the new website has received positive feedback from users and has seen increased traffic and engagement.
