Project overview

I was tasked with redesigning and improving the functionality of a website that was outdated and no longer meeting the client's needs. This was a similar task to a previous project that I had completed successfully.

In both cases, I utilized WordPress as the content management system and incorporated custom HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to add additional features and functionality. This included responsive design for mobile devices, improved site structure and navigation, integration of third-party plugins, custom widgets and shortcodes, and enhanced security measures.

Throughout both projects, I collaborated closely with the clients to ensure that the sites met their specific needs and goals. The end result was two modern, functional websites that received positive feedback from users and saw increased traffic and engagement.
